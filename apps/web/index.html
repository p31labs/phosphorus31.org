The Science

The Science

The Posner molecule — Ca₉(PO₄)₆ — is a calcium–phosphate cluster in bone. Matthew Fisher’s hypothesis proposes that phosphorus-31 nuclear spins in Posner molecules could maintain quantum coherence at room temperature, potentially supporting quantum processes in the brain.

³¹P magnetic resonance spectroscopy (³¹P MRS) can non-invasively probe phosphorus metabolism. The same isotope that defines P31 Labs may one day help us understand how the brain maintains coherence — and how to protect it.
