Mission

Cognitive prosthetics, not cognitive patches.

P31 Labs develops assistive technology that works with neurodivergent cognition instead of forcing neurotypical patterns. Our tools don't train you to mask. They amplify how you already think.

The name comes from Phosphorus-31 — the only stable isotope of phosphorus and the foundation of ATP, the molecule that powers every thought. Combined with calcium, it forms the Posner molecule: the hypothesized substrate for quantum cognition in biological systems.

We took that literally.
