The world we want

We believe that technology should be a liberating force — not one that surveils, excludes, or overwhelms. For too long, assistive tools have been afterthoughts, designed for the "average" user, leaving neurodivergent minds to adapt to ill‑fitting systems.

P31 Labs was founded to flip that paradigm. We build tools that listen to the body, that respect privacy, that connect us to each other without central control. Our work is rooted in science, but guided by a simple truth: the geometry protects the signal.

Our principles

🔺

Mesh Pattern

Resilience through decentralisation. Every node strengthens the mesh. No single point of failure, no central authority.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

Your data is yours. We build systems that never track, never profile, never sell. The mesh remembers only what it needs to route.

💚

Proof of Care

Value flows to those who nurture the network. Uptime, relays, and support are rewarded. An economy that incentivises generosity.

🧩

Universal Accessibility

Designed for neurodivergent minds, but usable by everyone. Screen readers, haptics, switch control — not add‑ons, but foundations.

A world connected, not controlled

Imagine a network that works when the internet goes down. A device that grounds you with gentle haptic pulses. A token you earn by helping others stay connected. This is not a distant dream — it's being built today, by a community of neurodivergent engineers, artists, and dreamers.

We are creating the P31 Compass: a routing protocol that learns the topology of care. The P31 Buffer: software that translates between bodies and machines. The P31 Spectrum: an interface that reveals the mesh without overwhelming. And at the centre, P31 NodeZero — a device you can hold, that holds you back.

"The only stable isotope is connection." — Will, founder
