WonkySprout Digital Centaur: Gemini Prompt Library
Copy and paste these into the "Help me write" (Docs/Gmail) or "Side Panel" (Sheets/Slides) prompts.
1. GMAIL: The "Executive Triage" Prompt
Goal: Summarize high-stakes threads and detect "Shadow IT" or security risks.
"Act as a Forensic Data Strategist. Analyze this email thread. 1) Summarize the core operational decision required. 2) Identify any mentioned third-party tools that might bypass our Shadow IT policy. 3) Draft a response that maintains our 'Backbone' data integrity while moving the project to Zone Beta (Operational)."
2. DOCS: The "SOP Architect" Prompt
Goal: Convert messy notes into immutable "Zone Alpha" assets.
"Act as a Senior Workspace Systems Architect. Review this document and rewrite it as a 'Zone Alpha' Standard Operating Procedure (SOP). Use a hierarchical structure (ISO 8601 dates, nested bullets). Ensure every instruction minimizes manual overhead and specifies where data must be stored within our Shared Drive Backbone."
3. SHEETS: The "Forensic Analyst" Prompt
Goal: Identifying anomalies and automating data flow.
"Analyze this dataset of [User Logs/Financials]. 1) Identify any outliers or timestamps that deviate from standard operational patterns. 2) Propose a formula to categorize these rows based on our 'Risk Level' (Low, Medium, High). 3) Generate an Apps Script snippet that would move 'High Risk' rows to a separate sheet for Admin review."
4. SLIDES: The "Strategic Visualizer" Prompt
Goal: Communicating technical infrastructure to non-technical stakeholders.
"I am presenting our new 'Geodesic Workspace' architecture. Create a 5-slide outline that explains: 1) Why we moved away from legacy 'My Drive' sprawl. 2) The security benefits of the Zone Alpha/Beta structure. 3) How our 'Reaper' script protects company data. 4) The ROI of automated offboarding. Keep the tone professional, authoritative, and secure."
5. DRIVE (SIDE PANEL): The "Librarian" Prompt
Goal: Finding specific data across the Backbone.
"Scan the 'Zone Alpha | Backbone' Shared Drive. Find the most recent SOP regarding [Project X]. Summarize the access permissions required for this project and list any conflicting documents found in 'Zone Gamma'."
