## APPENDIX: ASSISTIVE TECHNOLOGY ACT CONTEXT

The Assistive Technology Act of 1998 (as amended in 2004, Public Law 108-364) provides federal funding to states for AT programs. Georgia's Tools for Life receives this funding to:

- Increase access to AT devices through loans and demonstrations
- Provide training and technical assistance
- Conduct public awareness activities
- Support alternative financing programs
- Data collection on AT needs and outcomes

**Relevance:** Your consultation with Tools for Life establishes federal AT Act program awareness of DIY AT development need. This counters any argument that Phenix Navigator is "unnecessary" or "not legitimate AT"—the state program connected you to resources specifically for this purpose.
