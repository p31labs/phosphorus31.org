The foundational state of the Cognitive Jitterbug system is the Vector Equilibrium (VE), or cuboctahedron. It is crucial to understand that the VE represents the Pre-Cognitive State or the Void. It is not "nothingness" in the nihilistic sense, but rather the "Empty Theater" 2—a space of pure potentiality where all vectors are balanced and no specific action has yet been selected. In the context of the Operator's "Cognitive Shield," the VE corresponds to the "Green Zone" or "Zone 0" of regulation.4 It is the state of "Omnidirectional Attention," where the mind is open, receptive, and structurally balanced, but not yet engaged in the specific work of "Thinking."

In the UI specification, the VE is the "Dashboard" state. It is the "resting pulse" of the interface. If the VE is the potential for thought, the Tetrahedron is the Thought Itself. In the Cognitive Jitterbug architecture, a "Thought" is defined as a unit of information that has achieved Tetrahedral Integrity.
