Privacy Policy

P31 Labs ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our website, wallet, and hardware.

Data Collection

  • No tracking: We do not use analytics cookies, tracking pixels, or third‑party trackers. Our website is statically generated and does not log IP addresses.
  • Wallet: When you connect your wallet, we only read public blockchain data. We never store private keys.
  • Mesh network: P31 NodeZero devices exchange public keys and mesh routing information only; no personal data is transmitted.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics (e.g., number of active nodes) in public reports.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Since we collect almost no personal data, these rights are inherently respected. For any questions, contact privacy@p31labs.org.

Regulatory Compliance

Medical Device Status

P31 NodeZero is classified as a wellness device under 21 CFR § 890.3710 (Powered communication system). It is not a medical device and does not require FDA clearance. It is intended for general wellness, not to diagnose or treat any medical condition.

FCC / Industry Canada

P31 NodeZero uses a LoRa module that complies with FCC Part 15 and Industry Canada RSS‑247. Final certification is pending.

Export Controls

The software and hardware are subject to U.S. export controls. You may not export or re‑export them to countries under U.S. embargo.
