Mission

Cognitive prosthetics, not cognitive patches.

P31 Labs develops assistive technology that works with neurodivergent cognition instead of forcing neurotypical patterns. Our tools don't train you to mask. They amplify how you already think.

The name comes from Phosphorus-31, the only stable isotope of phosphorus and the foundation of ATP — the molecule that powers every thought. Combined with calcium, it forms the Posner molecule (Ca9(PO4)6), the hypothesized substrate for quantum cognition in biological systems.

We took that literally. Our product suite — hardware, software, and protocol — is designed from the molecular level up. Every interface decision is grounded in the neuroscience of how attention, executive function, and sensory processing actually work in divergent brains.

Products

Three layers. One organism.

P31 NodeZero
Hardware · ESP32-S3 · Open Source

An assistive wearable built on the ESP32-S3 microcontroller. Haptic feedback for sensory regulation, LoRa mesh communication for off-grid sovereignty, and a secure element (SE050) for hardware-backed identity. The "Thick Click" — every digital action has a physical recoil.

P31 Buffer
Software · Communication Processor

A communication processing layer for people whose brains overshoot in conversation. Voltage analysis, pattern detection, pause timing, friction logging. Not a filter — a lens. Helps you see your own signal before it transmits.

P31 Tandem
Protocol · Human-AI Collaboration

A structured protocol for human-AI collaboration where the human provides direction, context, and judgment while the AI provides memory and synthesis. Named for riding together. Built for people who think in systems but struggle with sequential output.
