MODULE 03

The Bridge

Honoring human limits.

The attention economy assumes infinite human energy. The Bridge rejects this. Built on Cognitive Load Theory and the community-derived Spoon Theory, it tracks a dual-currency ecosystem: spoons spent and love earned.

When energy is low, the system gracefully reduces demands. When connection happens, the ledger records it. L.O.V.E. tokens are soulbound — they cannot be bought, sold, or transferred. They can only be earned through care, creation, and consistency. The Bridge allows the operator to vest earned tokens directly to dependents, creating a permanent inheritance of verified parental effort that no algorithm can erase.

The Vault

Verifiable proof of presence.

When records matter, integrity is everything. The Vault is the physical manifestation of the Genesis Block — a system designed to protect verifiable truth.

Operating offline-first with AES-256-GCM encryption, the Vault secures every telemetry chain, every engagement record, every moment of connection on local hardware. MODULE 05

The Geodesic Room

Rigidity through triangulation.

Maxwell's rigidity criterion teaches that a structure becomes stable when its constraints match its degrees of freedom. The Geodesic Room applies this principle to cognitive architecture — each bond the operator forms (human, systemic, structural) is evaluated for its contribution to overall stability.

The room visualizes structural progression in real-time: which bonds carry load, which create redundancy, where the system is over-constrained or dangerously loose. It makes the invisible architecture of a life visible, navigable, and repairable.
