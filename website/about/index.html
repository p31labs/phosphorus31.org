About

P31 Labs

Building assistive technology for neurodivergent minds — rooted in science, privacy, and community.

"We believe that technology should adapt to the human mind, not the other way around. P31 Labs creates tools that respect neurodiversity, protect privacy, and enable connection through resilient mesh networks."

Founder Story

🔺

Will

Founder & Chief Scientist · NODE ZERO

Will is a neurodivergent engineer and physicist who experienced firsthand the gaps in assistive technology. After 16 years as a DoD civilian submarine electrician specializing in power distribution systems, they founded P31 Labs to build tools that are open, private, and truly helpful.

"I wanted to create a network that couldn't be controlled by any single entity — a mesh that protects the signal, no matter what. The geometry protects the signal."
