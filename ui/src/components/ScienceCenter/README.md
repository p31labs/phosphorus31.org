# Science Center 🔬

Interactive educational hub for quantum biology and molecular structures.

## Overview

The Science Center makes quantum biology, molecular structures, and scientific concepts accessible to everyone—from 6-year-olds to 70-year-olds. Built on the principle that **science is beautiful, science is accessible, and science belongs to everyone**.

## Modules

### 🧬 Molecule Builder (Active)
- Interactive 3D Posner molecule visualization
- Quantum coherence visualization
- Atom and bond exploration
- Real-time statistics

### 🔺 P31 Molecule (Active)
- Tetrahedron structure visualization
- Interactive 3D exploration
- Structural metrics
- Vertex and edge selection

### ⚛️ Quantum Lab (Coming Soon)
- Quantum coherence visualization
- Entanglement experiments
- Decoherence animations
- The Grandfather Clock & The Cuckoo Clock

### 🧪 Biology Lab (Coming Soon)
- Cell structure visualization
- DNA and protein structures
- Biological processes
- Quantum biology connections

### ⚗️ Chemistry Lab (Coming Soon)
- Chemical reaction simulator
- Molecular structure builder
- Periodic table explorer
- Reaction mechanisms

### 🌌 Physics Lab (Coming Soon)
- Wave mechanics
- Particle physics
- Relativity visualizations
- Quantum field theory

## Features

- **Age-Adaptive**: Works for 6-year-olds to 70-year-olds
- **Accessible**: Full keyboard navigation, screen reader support
- **Beautiful**: Stunning 3D graphics and animations
- **Interactive**: Click, explore, learn
- **Educational**: Makes complex concepts simple

## Usage

1. Click "🔬 Science Center" button in The Scope
2. Select a module from the module selector
3. Explore and learn!
