### V. LEGAL BASIS

**A. ADA Title II Requirements**
Title II of the ADA requires state courts to:
- Make reasonable modifications to policies, practices, and procedures when necessary to avoid discrimination
- Ensure effective communication with individuals with disabilities
- Provide auxiliary aids and services when necessary

**B. Case Law Support**
Courts have recognized that reasonable accommodations may include:
- Extended time for responses and decision-making
- Written communication preferences
- Modifications to standard procedures
- Continuances to accommodate disability-related needs

*See Tennessee v. Lane, 541 U.S. 509 (2004) (affirming ADA applies to courts)*
