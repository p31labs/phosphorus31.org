# What P31 Labs Needs from MATA

P31 Labs brings functional prototypes, not a pitch deck. The Scope (1,888 lines of production code) runs daily. Node One (ESP32-S3) has working firmware. The Buffer scans communication voltage in real time. We have the engineering. We need the ecosystem.

**Regulatory navigation:** We understand the FDA 510(k) Class II pathway conceptually, but need mentorship from someone who's navigated it. Node One is a sensory regulation device—assistive technology, not medical treatment, but the regulatory boundary matters. We need guidance on premarket submissions, quality systems, and clinical data requirements without over-engineering the process.

**User research access:** Our current beta pool is the founder plus family network. We need connections to AuDHD adults and their support systems who can test Node One in real-world scenarios—sensory overload in public spaces, executive function support during transitions, emergency communication when verbal capacity collapses.

**Institutional partnerships:** Schools, therapy practices, and disability services offices who would deploy Node One devices and provide structured feedback. We need partners who understand assistive technology procurement cycles and can validate the device in clinical or educational settings.

**Nonprofit structure:** We're transitioning from a solo founder with fiscal sponsorship (Hack Club HCB) to a functioning nonprofit with a board, grant writer, and clinical advisory. We need guidance on board composition, governance, and how to structure a nonprofit that can both sustain development and remain mission-locked.

**Network validation:** MATA's credibility opens doors with funders and partners who need third-party validation. We're seeking the specific connections that only an accelerator with MATA's track record can provide.

We don't need market validation. We live in the market. We don't need basic technical mentorship—we need domain-specific assistive technology and regulatory guidance. We don't need to find product-market fit—we need to scale what already works. The gap is the ecosystem, not the product.
