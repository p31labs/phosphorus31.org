## Legal and Procedural Violations

### Violation 1: Fraudulent Arrears Claim

**Issue:** The Plaintiff continued to pursue TSP liquidation despite arrears being resolved.

**Evidence:**
- Payments totaling $4,000 were made in October 2025
- Arrears were completely cleared by October 25, 2025
- Fraudulent court orders were filed on October 23, 2025

**Legal Impact:** This constitutes fraud upon the court and misrepresentation of material facts.

### Violation 2: Improper Use of Emergency Proceedings

**Issue:** Emergency proceedings were used despite no actual emergency.

**Evidence:**
- Arrears were being actively resolved
- No imminent foreclosure threat existed
- Plaintiff had adequate time to resolve the matter properly

**Legal Impact:** Abuse of court process and improper use of emergency procedures.

### Violation 3: Failure to Disclose Material Information

**Issue:** Plaintiff failed to disclose that arrears were being resolved.

**Evidence:**
- Payments were made but not disclosed to the court
- Plaintiff continued to represent arrears as unresolved
- Intentional concealment of payment information

**Legal Impact:** Violation of duty of candor to the court.
