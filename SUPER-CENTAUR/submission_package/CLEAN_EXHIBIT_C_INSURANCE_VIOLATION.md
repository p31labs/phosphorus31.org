### LEGAL VIOLATIONS

**Georgia Insurance Requirements (O.C.G.A. § 33-7-11):**
All motor vehicle owners must maintain minimum liability insurance coverage:
- Bodily Injury Liability: $25,000 per person / $50,000 per accident
- Property Damage Liability: $25,000 per accident

**Criminal Penalties:**
- First Offense: Up to $250 fine
- Second Offense: Up to $500 fine
- Third Offense: Up to $1,000 fine and/or 12 months imprisonment

**Civil Penalties:**
- Driver's License Suspension: 6 months to 3 years
- Vehicle Registration Suspension: Until insurance is obtained
- SR-22 Filing Requirement: For 3 years after reinstatement
