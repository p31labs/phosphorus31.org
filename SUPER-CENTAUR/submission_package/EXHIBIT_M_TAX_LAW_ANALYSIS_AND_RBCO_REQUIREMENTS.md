## Federal Tax Law Analysis

### Internal Revenue Code Section 72(t)

**Statutory Authority:**
26 U.S.C. § 72(t) imposes a 10% additional tax on early distributions from qualified retirement plans.

**General Rule:**
- **Applicability:** Applies to distributions before age 59½
- **Tax Rate:** 10% of the taxable amount distributed
- **Purpose:** Discourage premature withdrawal of retirement savings

### Statutory Exceptions

**26 U.S.C. § 72(t)(2) Exceptions:**
The 10% penalty does NOT apply to distributions that meet specific criteria:

**1. Section 72(t)(2)(C) - Domestic Relations Orders:**
> "Distributions made to an alternate payee under a qualified domestic relations order (as defined in section 414(p))"

**2. Section 72(t)(2)(A) - Age 59½:**
> "Distributions made on or after the date on which the employee attains age 59½"

**3. Section 72(t)(2)(B) - Death or Disability:**
> "Distributions made to a beneficiary (or to the estate of the employee) on or after the death of the employee, or on account of the employee's being disabled"

**4. Other Exceptions:**
- Medical expenses exceeding 7.5% of AGI
- Qualified higher education expenses
- First-time home purchase (up to $10,000)
- Substantially equal periodic payments
- Unreimbursed medical insurance while unemployed

## Retirement Benefits Court Order (RBCO) Requirements

### Legal Framework

**Governing Authority:**
- **5 U.S.C. § 8435(c):** TSP division in divorce proceedings
- **5 U.S.C. § 8467:** TSP court order requirements
- **5 C.F.R. Part 1653:** TSP court order regulations

### RBCO Definition

**Qualified Retirement Benefits Court Order:**
A court order that:
1. **Relates to Marital Property Rights:** Addresses division of marital property
2. **Identifies Plan and Parties:** Specifically names TSP and all parties
3. **Specifies Amount or Percentage:** Clearly states division terms
4. **Meets Timing Requirements:** Submitted within required timeframes

### TSP-Specific Requirements

**Essential Elements:**
1. **Plan Identification:** Must specifically name "Thrift Savings Plan"
2. **Party Identification:** Names of participant and payee(s)
3. **Amount Specification:** Dollar amount or percentage of account
4. **Calculation Date:** Date for determining account value
5. **Payment Method:** How distribution will be made

**Prohibited Elements:**
1. **Assignment Restrictions:** Cannot assign payee's rights
2. **Loan Responsibility:** Cannot assign loan repayment responsibility
3. **Excessive Amounts:** Cannot exceed participant's account balance

## TSP Distribution Tax Treatment

### General Tax Rules

**Traditional TSP Distributions:**
- **Taxable Income:** Entire distribution is taxable
- **Withholding:** 20% federal tax withholding required
- **Penalty:** 10% early withdrawal penalty if under age 59½

**Roth TSP Distributions:**
- **Contributions:** Tax-free (already taxed)
- **Earnings:** Taxable if not qualified distribution
- **Qualified Distribution:** Tax-free if held 5+ years and age 59½+

### RBCO Tax Treatment

**Distribution to Spouse/Former Spouse:**
- **Taxable Event:** Distribution is taxable to recipient
- **No Penalty:** 10% penalty does not apply under § 72(t)(2)(C)
- **Rollover Option:** Can roll over to IRA or eligible plan

**Distribution to Other Payees:**
- **Taxable Event:** Distribution is taxable to participant
- **Penalty May Apply:** 10% penalty may apply to participant
- **No Rollover:** Cannot roll over to other payees

## Qualified Domestic Relations Order (QDRO) Standards

### ERISA QDRO Requirements

**42 U.S.C. § 1056(d)(3) Definition:**
A QDRO is a judgment, decree, or order that:
1. **Relates to Marital Property:** Addresses marital property rights
2. **Creates Alternate Payee Rights:** Establishes rights to plan benefits
3. **Meets Specific Requirements:** Satisfies statutory criteria

### Required Elements

**Statutory Requirements:**
1. **Plan Identification:** Names the specific retirement plan
2. **Party Identification:** Identifies participant and alternate payee
3. **Benefit Description:** Describes benefits to be paid
4. **Payment Amount:** Specifies amount or percentage
5. **Payment Timing:** States when payments begin

### TSP RBCO Equivalency

**TSP Requirements Mirror QDRO Standards:**
The TSP's RBCO requirements are functionally equivalent to QDRO requirements for private plans.

**Key Similarities:**
1. **Plan Identification:** Must name specific plan
2. **Party Identification:** Must identify all parties
3. **Benefit Description:** Must describe benefits
4. **Legal Authority:** Must be court order
