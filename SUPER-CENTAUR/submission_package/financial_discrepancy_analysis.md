## Conclusion

The financial analysis reveals a clear mathematical impossibility in the claimed financial situation:

1. **Income Discrepancy:** Claimed monthly income of $3,200 is significantly lower than actual documented income
2. **Expense Manipulation:** Claimed expenses of $4,100 appear inflated or include non-essential discretionary spending
3. **Cell Phone Bill Support:** Financial support from unknown sources for cell phone bill totaling $2,670.71 during the analysis period
4. **Litigation Funding:** Ability to fund ongoing litigation while claiming financial hardship
