## COMMON REDACTION MISTAKES TO AVOID

### **MISTAKE 1: INCOMPLETE REDACTION**
❌ **WRONG:** Only covering first name, leaving last name visible
❌ **WRONG:** Using light marker that allows text to show through
❌ **WRONG:** Missing instances of children's names in footnotes or appendices

✅ **CORRECT:** Complete coverage of entire name with opaque material
✅ **CORRECT:** Multiple layers if necessary to ensure opacity
✅ **CORRECT:** Thorough search of entire document including attachments

### **MISTAKE 2: INCONSISTENT REDACTION**
❌ **WRONG:** Sometimes using "S.J." and sometimes "Sebastian J."
❌ **WRONG:** Redacting some addresses but not others
❌ **WRONG:** Inconsistent formatting of redacted information

✅ **CORRECT:** Consistent use of "S.J." and "W.J." throughout
✅ **CORRECT:** Uniform redaction of all identifying information
✅ **CORRECT:** Standardized format for all redacted documents

### **MISTAKE 3: OVER-REDACATION**
❌ **WRONG:** Redacting information that doesn't identify children
❌ **WRONG:** Removing information necessary for legal arguments
❌ **WRONG:** Making documents unreadable or unusable

✅ **CORRECT:** Redact only children's identifying information
✅ **CORRECT:** Preserve all legally relevant information
✅ **CORRECT:** Maintain document readability and usability
