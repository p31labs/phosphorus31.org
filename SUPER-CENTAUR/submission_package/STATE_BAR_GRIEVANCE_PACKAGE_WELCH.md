## 1. GRIEVANCE SUMMARY

### Nature of Grievance: ABANDONMENT & BREACH OF FIDUCIARY DUTY

Attorney J. Alan Welch engaged in systematic professional misconduct including:

1. **Client Abandonment During Medical Crisis** - Withdrew during documented medical emergency
2. **Breach of Fiduciary Duty** - Failed to protect client's financial interests
3. **Coercion and Duress** - Attempted to force client into detrimental agreements
4. **Professional Negligence** - Failed to act competently and diligently

### Financial Impact: $77,000+ in Direct Losses

The misconduct resulted in catastrophic financial harm to the client through forced liquidation of retirement accounts and abandonment during critical medical crisis.

## 2. PROFESSIONAL MISCONDUCT ALLEGATIONS

### 2.1 Abandonment During Medical Crisis (Primary Violation)

**Incident Date:** January 2026

**Allegation:**
Attorney Welch abandoned his client during a documented, life-threatening medical crisis.

**Medical Condition:** Hypoparathyroidism with "Medical Blackout" (Calcium 7.8 mg/dL)
**Legal Status:** Client was experiencing medical incapacity due to stress and lack of medication
**Welch's Action:** Withdrew from representation despite knowing client's medical condition

**Evidence:**
- **January 2026:** Client informed Welch of medical crisis
- **Client's Condition:** Hypocalcemia causing cognitive impairment
- **Welch's Response:** Withdrew from case during medical emergency
- **Impact:** Left client without representation during critical proceedings

**Legal Violation:** Abandonment under Georgia Rules of Professional Conduct Rule 1.16

### 2.2 Breach of Fiduciary Duty

**Incident Date:** October 2025 - January 2026

**Allegation:**
Attorney Welch breached his fiduciary duty to his client by:

1. **Failing to Protect Client's Financial Interests:** Did not prevent forced liquidation
2. **Failing to Act Diligently:** Did not take necessary protective actions
3. **Failing to Provide Competent Representation:** Allowed client to suffer catastrophic losses
4. **Improper Fee Refund:** Refunded only 50% of unearned fees

**Financial Impact:** $77,000+ in direct financial losses

### 2.3 Coercion and Duress

**Incident Date:** October 30, 2025

**Allegation:**
Attorney Welch attempted to coerce his client into signing a detrimental consent order.

**Evidence:**
- **October 30, 2025:** Welch forwarded "2nd Consent Order" from opposing counsel
- **Order Content:** Would have validated fraudulent seizure of client's assets
- **Client's Response:** Refused to sign the detrimental agreement
- **Welch's Action:** Attempted to pressure client into accepting harmful terms

**Legal Violation:** Breach of duty of loyalty and good faith

### 2.4 Professional Negligence

**Incident Date:** October 2025 - January 2026

**Allegation:**
Attorney Welch failed to provide competent and diligent representation by:

1. **Failing to Protect Client Assets:** Did not prevent forced liquidation
2. **Failing to Act Timely:** Did not take necessary actions to protect client
3. **Failing to Communicate:** Did not adequately inform client of options
4. **Failing to Withdraw Properly:** Abandoned client during medical crisis
