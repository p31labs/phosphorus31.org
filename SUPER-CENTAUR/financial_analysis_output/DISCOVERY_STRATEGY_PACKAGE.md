## LEGAL STRATEGY DOCUMENTS

### 1. MOTION TO COMPEL DISCOVERY

#### Grounds for Motion:
1. **Incomplete Financial Disclosure** - Failure to disclose all assets and income
2. **Unresponsive Interrogatories** - Vague or incomplete answers to discovery requests
3. **Withheld Documents** - Failure to produce requested financial records
4. **Financial Misrepresentation** - Inconsistent financial statements

#### Requested Relief:
1. **Order Compelling Discovery** - Require complete responses to all discovery requests
2. **Sanctions** - Award attorney fees and costs for discovery violations
3. **Adverse Inference** - Instruct jury to infer facts from withheld evidence
4. **Default Judgment** - On financial issues due to discovery violations

**Legal Authority:** Georgia Code § 9-11-37 (Failure to Make Discovery)

---

### 2. MOTION FOR SANCTIONS

#### Basis for Sanctions:
1. **False Financial Affidavit** - Knowingly false statements under oath
2. **Discovery Violations** - Failure to comply with discovery rules
3. **Fraud on the Court** - Intentional concealment of financial information
4. **Waste of Judicial Resources** - Unnecessary litigation due to false statements

#### Requested Sanctions:
1. **Attorney Fees** - All fees incurred due to financial misconduct
2. **Punitive Damages** - For willful concealment of assets
3. **Dismissal of Claims** - Financial support and property division claims
4. **Contempt of Court** - For false statements under oath

**Legal Authority:** Georgia Code § 9-11-11 (Sanctions for False Statements)
