## COURT PROCEDURES (General Guidance)

### What to Expect

1. **Check-in:** Arrive early, check in with clerk
2. **Wait:** May wait in hallway or courtroom
3. **Hearing:** Judge will call case
4. **Presentation:** Present your case factually
5. **Questions:** Judge may ask questions
6. **Ruling:** Judge may rule immediately or take under advisement

### How to Present Yourself

- **Dress:** Professional (business casual minimum)
- **Behavior:** Respectful, calm, factual
- **Language:** Clear, direct, avoid emotional language
- **Documents:** Organized, easy to reference
- **Time:** Be concise, respect court's time

### What to Say

**Opening:**
> "Your Honor, I'm [name], appearing pro se. I'm here to [purpose of hearing]."

**Key Points:**
- State facts, not emotions
- Reference exhibits when relevant
- Be concise and clear
- Answer questions directly

**Closing:**
> "Your Honor, I believe the evidence shows [summary]. I respectfully request [what you're asking for]."
