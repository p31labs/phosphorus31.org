# P31 Labs — 1-Minute Pitch
**Multiple Autism Tech Accelerator · Demo Day**

---

## THE HOOK (10 seconds)
1 in 36 children are diagnosed autistic. Most assistive technology is designed BY neurotypical people FOR neurotypical assumptions. Late-diagnosed adults fall through every crack.

## THE PROBLEM (15 seconds)
When systems fail — divorce, job loss, benefits disruption — neurodivergent people lose their ground reference. There's no technology layer to catch them. Existing tools don't account for sensory sensitivity, executive function challenges, or communication differences.

## THE SOLUTION (20 seconds)
P31 Labs builds a three-layer assistive technology stack:
- **Node One:** ESP32-S3 hardware for sensory regulation and emergency communication
- **The Buffer:** Communication voltage scanner that holds high-pressure messages
- **The Scope:** Production dashboard tracking medications, energy, and system alerts

Everything works offline. No cloud dependency. Delta topology — no single point of failure.

## THE TRACTION (10 seconds)
1,888 lines of production code. Working ESP32-S3 prototype. Gemini AI integration complete. Defensive publications on Zenodo. Georgia Tech Tools for Life partnership.

## THE ASK (5 seconds)
Go-to-market strategy for Class II medical device. Hardware manufacturing partnerships. Regulatory mentorship. Network access to autism tech ecosystem.
