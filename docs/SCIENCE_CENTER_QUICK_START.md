## What Is the Science Center?

An **interactive educational hub** that makes quantum biology, molecular structures, and scientific concepts accessible to everyone—from 6-year-olds to 70-year-olds.

**The Centerpiece:** The P31 Molecule Builder — interactive 3D visualization of Posner molecules (Ca9(PO4)6) containing Phosphorus-31, the biological qubit.

## Molecule Builder (Active)

### What You'll See

- **3D Posner Molecule** — Ca9(PO4)6 structure rotating in space
- **P31 Atoms** — Orange-red atoms pulsing with quantum coherence
- **Quantum Effects** — Coherence rings, quantum glow, pulsing
- **Real-time Stats** — Coherence level, entanglement, phase

### Controls

- **Click and drag** — Rotate molecule
- **Scroll** — Zoom in/out
- **Click atom** — See details (element, charge, quantum properties)
- **Toggle quantum** — Show/hide quantum effects
- **Toggle labels** — Show/hide element labels

### What to Explore

1. **Click P31 atoms** — See the biological qubit in action
2. **Watch quantum effects** — See coherence rings pulse
3. **Rotate molecule** — Explore from all angles
4. **Read atom details** — Learn about each element
5. **Generate new molecule** — Create fresh Posner structures
