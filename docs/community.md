# P31 Community Guide

Building community around P31 - with love and light, as above so below.

## Welcome to the P31 Community

P31 is more than code—it's a community of people building something meaningful together. This guide helps you connect, contribute, and grow with the community.

## Community Values

### 1. Respect

- Treat all community members with respect
- Value diverse perspectives and experiences
- Listen before responding
- Assume good intentions

### 2. Privacy

- Respect user privacy above all
- Don't share private information
- Use encryption by default
- Protect the mesh

### 3. Sovereignty

- Code for departure
- No vendor lock-in
- Build for independence
- Support self-hosting

### 4. Accessibility

- Universal accessibility is a requirement
- Support all abilities
- Document clearly
- Make it easy

### 5. Love and Light

- Build with care
- Support each other
- Share knowledge freely
- Grow together

## Code of Conduct

### Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in P31 a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### Our Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct inappropriate for a professional setting

### Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
