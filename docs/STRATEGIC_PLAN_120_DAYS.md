## Structural Decision: LLC vs Nonprofit vs Hybrid

### The Core Issue

**As an LLC:**
- Donations are taxable business income
- Not tax-deductible for donors
- Cannot access crypto philanthropy ecosystem (The Giving Block: $1B+ processed, $10,978 average donation)
- High-net-worth crypto donors seek tax-advantaged giving

### Solutions

**Option 1: Fiscal Sponsorship (Bridge)**
- Partner with established 501(c)(3) fiscal sponsor
- Continue operating as LLC
- Accept tax-deductible donations through sponsor
- 5-10% fee typical
- **Open Collective**: Best fit for P31 (transparency, budgeting, tax handling)

**Option 2: Companion 501(c)(3)**
- Form separate nonprofit entity
- LLC handles business operations
- 501(c)(3) handles donations
- More complex, but full control

**Option 3: Hybrid Model**
- Keep LLC for business operations
- Use fiscal sponsorship for donations
- Maintain flexibility

### Recommendation

**Start with Open Collective fiscal sponsorship** as immediate bridge. Evaluate 501(c)(3) formation after:
- First 6 months of donation activity
- Clear understanding of donor base
- Revenue projections from hybrid streams
