# P31 Molecule Builder Hero

**The centerpiece of P31 - The biological qubit visualized**

## Overview

The P31 Molecule Builder Hero is the stunning centerpiece of the P31 ecosystem. It's a beautiful, immersive 3D interface for exploring Posner molecules (Ca9(PO4)6) containing Phosphorus-31 - the nuclear spin that carries quantum coherence in biological systems.

## Features

### Hero Interface
- **Gradient Header** - Beautiful cyan-to-orange gradient title
- **Real-time Stats** - Quantum coherence, P31 count, total atoms, bonds
- **Split Layout** - Large 3D canvas with side control panel
- **Auto-rotation** - Smooth automatic rotation (respects reduced motion)
- **Quantum Grid** - Cyan grid that pulses with quantum energy

### 3D Visualization
- **High-quality Rendering** - 32-segment spheres, smooth lighting
- **Quantum Effects** - P31 atoms pulse with coherence
- **Coherence Rings** - Cyan rings show quantum state
- **Quantum Glow** - P31 atoms have additional glow effect
- **Bond Visualization** - Properly rotated cylinders between atoms
- **Floating Label** - Ca9(PO4)6 formula displayed in 3D space

### Control Panel
- **New Molecule Button** - Generate fresh Posner molecules
- **Toggle Controls** - Quantum coherence and labels
- **Atom Details** - Click atoms to see full information
- **Molecule Info** - Formula, geometry, coherence lifetime
- **Quantum Details** - Coherence, spin, entanglement info

### Visual Polish
- **Gradient Backgrounds** - Deep space gradients
- **Glass Morphism** - Backdrop blur effects
- **Glowing Borders** - Cyan borders with glow
- **Smooth Animations** - Respects reduced motion preference
- **Responsive Design** - Adapts to screen size

## Usage

### Opening
1. Click **"🧬 P31 Molecule Builder"** button in The Scope
2. The hero interface opens in full-screen
3. A Posner molecule is automatically generated

### Interaction
- **Rotate** - Click and drag to orbit
- **Zoom** - Scroll wheel
- **Select Atom** - Click any atom to see details
- **Toggle Quantum** - Checkbox to show/hide quantum effects
- **Toggle Labels** - Checkbox to show/hide element labels

### Controls
- **New Posner Molecule** - Generates a fresh Ca9(PO4)6 structure
- **Quantum Coherence** - Visualize quantum states
- **Element Labels** - Show/hide atom labels

### Components
