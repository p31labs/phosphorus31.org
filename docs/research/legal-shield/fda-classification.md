## Research Prompt (For Reference)

```
Research the FDA regulatory classification of powered communication systems and augmentative/alternative communication (AAC) devices under 21 CFR Part 890, specifically § 890.3710. I need:

1. The exact regulatory text and classification history of "Powered Communication Systems"
2. Which devices have been classified under this regulation (trade names, manufacturers, 510(k) numbers if exempt)
3. The distinction between Class I and Class II exempt devices in this category
4. Case studies of devices that received Class II exemption without 510(k) premarket notification
5. How the FDA defines "intended use" for communication aids vs. general consumer electronics
6. Any FDA guidance documents on software as a medical device (SaMD) that applies to communication aids
7. The intersection of FDA classification with ADA assistive technology definitions

Focus on devices intended for cognitive or communication disabilities, not just speech-generating devices. Include any devices designed for executive function support, sensory regulation, or cognitive processing assistance.
```
