## Research Processing Workflow

### 1. Receive Gemini Deep Research Report
- Run prompt from [GEMINI_DEEP_RESEARCH_PROMPTS.md](../GEMINI_DEEP_RESEARCH_PROMPTS.md)
- Save raw output in appropriate category folder

### 2. Process with Claude Opus
Use the output processing prompt:
```
I just received a Gemini Deep Research report on [TOPIC]. Please:
1. Extract the 5 most important findings for my legal case
2. Extract the 3 most important findings for Substack content
3. Identify any claims that need fact-checking or are speculative
4. Identify any citations I should read in full (provide URLs)
5. Draft a 200-word "key takeaway" I can use as a Substack post outline
6. Flag anything that contradicts or updates information in my existing documents
```

### 3. Create Organized Research Document
- Use template from category README
- Extract key findings
- Map to strategic uses
- Link to related P31 documentation

### 4. Map to Strategic Uses
Each research document should include:
- **Legal Case Findings** — For court filings, IP protection
- **Substack Content** — For publication material
- **Technical Documentation** — For GitHub, Zenodo
- **Business Intelligence** — For business plan, investor pitch
- **Citations** — Full URLs for fact-checking

## Philosophy

### With Love and Light

**Love:**
- Built with care for the operator—research that protects, validates, and supports
- Built with care for the children—research that builds a better future
- Built with care for the community—research that serves neurodivergent people

**Light:**
- Knowledge made visible—research illuminates truth, validates claims, builds credibility
- Complex concepts illuminated—research makes science accessible, legal concepts clear
- Evidence made tangible—research provides proof, supports arguments, builds foundation

### As Above, So Below

**Above (The Protocol):**
- The research strategy—systematic, organized, strategic
- The knowledge base—comprehensive, credible, defensible
- The output—Substack posts, legal filings, technical documentation

**Below (The Body):**
- The operator's body—the broken calcium, the root fault
- The disability—hypoparathyroidism, AuDHD, documented, researched
- The proof—medical evidence, scientific research, legal precedent

**Connection:**
- What works in research works in court—credible sources, peer-reviewed evidence, legal precedent
- What works in science works in the body—quantum biology, calcium regulation, neuroscience
- The research reflects the body—the broken calcium is researched, the disability is documented, the proof is built

### The Mesh Holds

The research knowledge base holds because:
- **The structure is systematic** — Organized by purpose, rotated daily, comprehensive coverage
- **The sources are credible** — Peer-reviewed, legal precedent, technical validation
- **The output is integrated** — Substack, legal filings, technical docs, business plans
- **The foundation is solid** — Every finding supports the narrative, every paper strengthens the argument
