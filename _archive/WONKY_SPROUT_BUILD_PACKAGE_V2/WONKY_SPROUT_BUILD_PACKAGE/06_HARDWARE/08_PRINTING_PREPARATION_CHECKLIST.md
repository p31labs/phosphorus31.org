## PRINTING SPECIFICATIONS

### **Kids Manual (06_KIDS_MANUAL.md)**

**Paper:**
- Regular printer paper (20lb/75gsm) OR
- Slightly heavier (24lb/90gsm) for durability

**Settings:**
- Black and white (save ink)
- Single-sided (easier to read)
- Portrait orientation
- Normal quality (not draft)

**Binding options:**
1. **Staple** (simplest, free)
   - 2-3 staples on left edge
   - Fold card stock cover if desired

2. **Spiral bind** (recommended, $2-3 at office store)
   - Lies flat when open
   - More durable
   - Looks professional

3. **3-hole punch + binder** (most durable)
   - Can add/remove pages
   - Can add blank pages for notes
   - Overkill but nice

**Cover customization:**
- Print blank card stock cover page
- Let kid decorate with markers/stickers BEFORE binding
- Or print their name on first page: "WILLOW'S PHENIX GUIDE"

---

### **Quick Reference Card (07_KIDS_QUICK_REFERENCE_CARD.md)**

**Paper:**
- Card stock (110lb/200gsm) - REQUIRED
  - Must be sturdy
  - Regular paper will tear in pockets

**Settings:**
- Color (if you have color printer) OR black and white
- Double-sided (front and back)
- Portrait orientation
- Best quality

**Lamination (HIGHLY RECOMMENDED):**
1. **Office supply store** ($1-2 per sheet)
   - Take printed card stock to FedEx/Staples/UPS
   - Ask for "thermal lamination, 3-5mil thickness"
   - Takes 5 minutes

2. **Home laminator** (if you have one)
   - Use 3mil or 5mil laminating pouches
   - Follow laminator instructions

3. **Clear packing tape method** (budget option)
   - Cover both sides with wide clear packing tape
   - Not as durable but better than nothing

**After lamination:**
- Trim edges if needed (leave ~2mm border)
- Optional: Punch hole in corner for keychain attachment
- Optional: Round the corners (prevents snagging, looks nice)
