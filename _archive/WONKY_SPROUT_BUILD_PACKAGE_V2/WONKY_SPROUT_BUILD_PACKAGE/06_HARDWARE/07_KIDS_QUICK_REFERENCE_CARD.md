## 🖨️ PRINTING INSTRUCTIONS

**For grown-ups preparing this card:**

1. **Print this document** on cardstock (heavy paper) or regular paper

2. **Laminate it** at office supply store or with a home laminator
   - Makes it waterproof
   - Prevents wear and tear
   - Lasts through kid handling

3. **Optional: Fold in half** to make 2-sided pocket card
   - Front = Basic usage
   - Back = Troubleshooting & codes

4. **Optional: Punch hole and add to keychain**
   - Attach to device lanyard
   - Always accessible

5. **Optional: Print multiples**
   - One stays with device
   - One in backpack
   - One in each parent's house

---

## 📏 ALTERNATE SIZES

### **Wallet Size (Credit Card)**
Scale to 3.375" × 2.125" (85.6mm × 54mm)
- Fits in wallet
- Very portable
- Smaller text (harder for young kids to read)

### **Index Card Size**
Scale to 5" × 3" (127mm × 76mm)
- Good middle ground
- Easy to read
- Fits in pocket

### **Full Page**
Keep at 8.5" × 11" (letter size)
- Largest text (easiest to read)
- Can post on wall next to charging station
- Not portable

## 🎨 CUSTOMIZATION IDEAS

**Personalize for each kid:**

1. **Add their name** at the top
   - "BASH'S PHENIX GUIDE"
   - Makes it feel special

2. **Replace generic labels** with actual family names
   - Instead of "Dad" → "Will" or "Daddy"
   - Instead of "Sister" → "Willow"

3. **Add their device color** to the illustration
   - If case is blue, color the drawing blue
   - Visual association

4. **Add photo of their actual device**
   - Take clear photo
   - Print next to the diagram
   - Helps identify THEIR specific unit

5. **Highlight THEIR button**
   - Circle or star the button position
   - "This is YOUR button on other people's devices"

6. **Add stickers**
   - Let kids decorate laminated card with stickers
   - Ownership + personalization
