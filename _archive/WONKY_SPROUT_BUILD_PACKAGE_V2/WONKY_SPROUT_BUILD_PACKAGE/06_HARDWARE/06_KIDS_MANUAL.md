# MY PHENIX NAVIGATOR
## A Guide for Kids

**This device is YOURS. This guide teaches you how to use it.**

---

## WHAT IS THIS THING?

Your Phenix Navigator is a **family walkie-talkie** that works without WiFi, without cell towers, and without the internet.

**It uses radio waves** (like invisible lasers) to send messages to your family.

**It's called Phenix** because phoenixes are birds that can always find their way home, just like these messages always find your family.

---

## THE MOST IMPORTANT RULE

**🔴 RED BUTTON = SPACE**

If you ever feel overwhelmed, scared, or just need everyone to STOP talking to you:

**Press the big red button on the bottom corner.**

Your device will tell everyone: **"I need space right now."**

They'll still get your messages, but they won't bug you until you're ready.

**You can press it whenever you need to. That's what it's for.**

---

## THE FOUR BUTTONS = YOUR FOUR PEOPLE

Your device has four buttons in the corners. **Each button is a person in your family.**

```
    [Dad]           [Mom]
    (top left)      (top right)



    [Willow]        [Bash]
    (bottom left)   (bottom right)
```

**To send a message to someone: Press their button.**

That's it. Press Dad's button, send a message to Dad. Press Bash's button, send a message to Bash.

**Your finger touches their corner, your message goes to them.**

---

## HOW TO TURN IT ON (WAKING IT UP)

Your Phenix sleeps at night, just like you. In the morning, you wake it up.

### **STEP 1: UNLOCK THE RED BUTTON**

The big red button on the bottom corner is locked down (twisted).

**Twist it to the left (counterclockwise) until it POPS UP.**

You'll hear a **CLICK**. That's the unlock sound.

---

### **STEP 2: FLIP UP THE ANTENNA**

On the bottom left corner, there's a little black stick (the antenna). It's folded flat.

**Flip it UP until it stands straight.**

You'll feel it **CLICK** when it's all the way up.

*Why?* The antenna listens for your family's messages. It needs to be up to hear them.

---

### **STEP 3: TURN ON THE POWER**

Find the power switch (usually on the side or back).

**Slide it to ON** or **press the power button**.

Your device will **BUZZ** once. That means it's waking up.

---

### **STEP 4: WAIT FOR IT TO FIND YOUR FAMILY**

The screen will light up and show you:

```
Searching for family...
```

Watch the four corner lights:
- **Dad's corner** (top left) glows BLUE when it finds Dad
- **Mom's corner** (top right) glows PURPLE when it finds Mom
- **Your sister/brother's corner** glows when it finds them

When all four corners are glowing, you'll hear a **CHORD** (a happy sound).

That means **your whole family is connected!**

---

### **STEP 5: YOU'RE READY**

The screen will say:

```
READY
```

Now you can send messages!

---

## HOW TO SEND A MESSAGE

### **THE QUICK WAY (EMOJI):**

**1. Press the button for the person you want to talk to**

Example: Press Bash's button (bottom right, green)

**2. The screen shows pictures (emojis)**

You'll see things like:
- ❤️ (I love you)
- 👋 (Hi!)
- 🍕 (I'm hungry)
- ✅ (Okay!)
- 🆘 (Help!)

**3. Press the emoji you want**

Touch the screen on the emoji.

**4. Press their button AGAIN to send**

Press Bash's button one more time.

**DONE!** Bash's device will light up and show your message.

---

### **THE TALKING WAY (WORDS):**

If you want to type words instead of just emojis:

**1. Press the person's button**

**2. Choose "Type Message"** on the screen

**3. Use the alphabet on the screen to spell**

(This is slower, but you can say exactly what you want)

**4. Press "SEND"**

Your message goes!

## HOW TO SEE WHO SENT YOU A MESSAGE

**Your device will GLOW when someone sends you a message.**

The **color** tells you who it's from:
- **Blue glow** = Dad sent a message
- **Purple glow** = Mom sent a message
- **Red glow** = Willow sent a message
- **Green glow** = Bash sent a message

**Just tap the screen** and you'll see what they said.

## THE COLOR LANGUAGE (SECRET CODES)

You can send messages with just **colors** - no words needed!

Here's what the colors mean:

- **💗 PINK** = I love you
- **💚 GREEN** = I'm safe / I'm okay
- **🧡 ORANGE** = I'm hungry
- **💙 BLUE** = I'm tired / sleepy
- **❤️ RED (flashing)** = HELP! Emergency!
- **💛 YELLOW (blinking)** = I'm sending a message right now
- **🤍 WHITE (dim)** = I'm here but quiet

**To send a color:**
1. Press someone's button
2. Choose "Send Color"
3. Pick the color
4. Send!

Their device will glow that color, and they'll know how you're feeling.

## THE SPECIAL BUTTONS

### **🔼🔽 ROCKER SWITCH (left side, middle)**

This little switch rocks up and down.

**Press UP** = Scroll up on the screen  
**Press DOWN** = Scroll down on the screen

Use it to look through your messages or pick things from a list.

---

### **⚙️ KNOB (right side, middle)**

The round spinning thing is called a "knob" or "encoder."

**Spin it** = Scroll slowly and carefully  
**Press it** = Select (like clicking)

It's good for when you need to pick something exactly right.

---

### **🔴 BIG RED BUTTON (bottom right corner)**

**THIS IS YOUR "I NEED SPACE" BUTTON.**

Press it when:
- Too many messages coming in
- You're overwhelmed
- You need quiet time
- You're upset and need a break

**What happens:**
- Screen says "SPACE MODE"
- Messages still come but don't make noise
- Your device glows slow red (breathing light)
- Your family sees "I need space right now"

**To turn it off:**
- Twist the button to unlock it
- Press it again
- Back to normal!

## THE EMERGENCY BUTTON (ALL FOUR CORNERS)

**If you're in real danger or really need help:**

**Put both hands on your device and SQUEEZE all four corner buttons at the same time.**

```
Left hand presses:           Right hand presses:
- Dad's button (top)         - Mom's button (top)
- Willow's button (bottom)   - Bash's button (bottom)
```

**This sends an EMERGENCY message to EVERYONE.**

Their devices will:
- Flash RED
- Make a loud alarm sound
- Show where you are (GPS location)
- Keep buzzing until they check

**Only use this for real emergencies:**
- You're lost
- You're hurt
- Someone's bothering you
- You're really scared

**NOT for:**
- You're bored
- You want attention
- You think it's funny

**It's like calling 911 - only when you really need help.**

## HOW TO TURN IT OFF (PUTTING IT TO SLEEP)

At night or when you're done using it, put your Phenix to sleep:

### **STEP 1: HOLD THE POWER BUTTON**

Press and hold the power button for **3 seconds** (count: one-Mississippi, two-Mississippi, three-Mississippi).

Screen will ask: **"Power off?"**

Press **YES**.

---

### **STEP 2: WATCH IT SAY GOODBYE**

Your device will:
- Send "Goodnight!" to everyone
- Show each person's name as it disconnects
- Play a soft goodnight sound
- Tell you to fold the antenna and lock the red button

---

### **STEP 3: FOLD THE ANTENNA**

Push the antenna down flat against the bottom edge.

You'll hear a **CLICK** when it's locked in place.

---

### **STEP 4: LOCK THE RED BUTTON**

Push the big red button DOWN and twist it to the right (clockwise).

It should lock in the down position.

---

### **STEP 5: CHARGE IT (OPTIONAL)**

Plug the USB-C cable into the bottom of your device (middle).

Plug the other end into a charger or computer.

A little light will glow to show it's charging.

**Leave it charging overnight** so it's ready in the morning!

## TAKING CARE OF YOUR PHENIX

### **DO:**
- ✅ Charge it every night
- ✅ Keep it dry (it's not waterproof)
- ✅ Fold the antenna when you put it in your pocket
- ✅ Lock the red button when you're not using it
- ✅ Wipe the screen with a soft cloth if it gets dirty

### **DON'T:**
- ❌ Drop it (it's handmade and can break)
- ❌ Get it wet (especially don't put it in water)
- ❌ Leave it in a hot car (battery can swell)
- ❌ Pull hard on the antenna (it can break off)
- ❌ Open it up (you'll break it and it's not safe inside)

## TROUBLESHOOTING (WHEN IT DOESN'T WORK)

### **"My Phenix won't turn on!"**

**Try this:**
1. Is the red button unlocked? (Twist it to pop it up)
2. Is it charged? (Plug it in for 10 minutes, try again)
3. Is the power switch ON? (Slide it the other way)

Still won't turn on? Tell a grown-up.

---

### **"I can't send messages!"**

**Try this:**
1. Is your antenna up? (Flip it vertical)
2. Are you close enough to the other person? (They need to be less than 500 meters away - that's about 5 football fields)
3. Is the other person's device turned on?

Still not working? Try turning your device off and on again.

---

### **"The screen won't let me tap things!"**

**Try this:**
1. Wipe the screen with your shirt (might be dirty)
2. Turn it off and on again
3. Make sure your fingers are dry (wet fingers don't work well)

Still frozen? Tell a grown-up.

---

### **"My Phenix is getting hot!"**

**STOP using it and tell a grown-up RIGHT AWAY.**

Unplug it if it's charging. Let it cool down.

If it smells weird or you see smoke, **PRESS THE RED BUTTON** and put it outside on concrete or dirt (not on carpet or wood).

---

### **"I dropped it and now it's broken!"**

**Tell a grown-up.** They might be able to fix it, or they'll know what to do.

Don't try to fix it yourself - there are tiny wires and batteries inside that can be dangerous.

## THE TETRAHEDRON THING (WHY IT'S SPECIAL)

Your dad built this device to teach you about something called a **tetrahedron**.

A tetrahedron is a 3D shape with **four points** (like a pyramid).

**Your family is like a tetrahedron:**
- **Four people** = Four points
- **Connected together** = Stronger than any person alone
- **Even if one person is far away**, the others hold the shape together

**The four buttons on your device are the four points.**

When you press Dad's button, you're touching his point in the family shape.

When all four of you are connected (all four corner lights glowing), that's the **complete tetrahedron** - your whole family working together.

**Even when Mom and Dad don't live in the same house**, the tetrahedron still works because you and your brother/sister connect everyone.

**That's why this device is special.**

It's not just a walkie-talkie. It's a way to **keep your family strong** even when things change.

## QUESTIONS KIDS ASK

### **"Can other people see my messages?"**

Nope! Your messages are **encrypted** (that means scrambled). Only your family's devices can unscramble them.

It's like a secret code that only your family knows.

---

### **"What if I lose it?"**

Tell a grown-up right away. They might be able to find it by sending it messages and listening for the beeps.

Also, your dad probably built more than one, so you might get a backup.

---

### **"Can I change the colors and sounds?"**

Maybe! Ask your dad. Some settings can be changed, some can't.

---

### **"Does it work everywhere?"**

It works **without WiFi or cell towers**, so it works in places phones don't!

But you need to be within about **500 meters** (5 football fields) of the other person.

If you're farther away, the message might **hop through** another family member's device to reach them. That's the "mesh network" thing your dad talks about.

---

### **"Why is it called Phenix?"**

A phoenix is a magical bird that:
- Always finds its way home
- Can't be destroyed (it comes back to life)
- Is loyal to its family

Your dad named these devices Phenix because:
- Messages always find your family
- Even if one device breaks, the others keep working
- You're connected no matter what

---

### **"Can I decorate it?"**

Ask your dad! The case is clear plastic, so stickers might show through weird.

But you could:
- Put stickers on the BACK
- Draw on the case with dry-erase markers (wipes off)
- Add a cool lanyard or charm
- Ask your dad to print a different color case
