### Key Diagnostic Gap

| Period | Duration | Conditions Present | Conditions Diagnosed |
|--------|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| 2003–2025 | 22 years | Hypoparathyroidism + ADHD + Autism | Hypoparathyroidism only |
| 2025–present | 1 year | All three conditions | All three conditions |

**The 22-year gap between condition onset and neurodevelopmental diagnosis is the single most important fact in this timeline.** It explains why no accommodations were sought, why the work history appears functional, and why the collapse occurred when it did. SSR 16-3p explicitly requires adjudicators to consider diagnostic delay as a factor, not a disqualifier.
