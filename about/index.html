About P31 Labs

Building assistive technology for neurodivergent minds. Open source. Offline-first. The mesh holds.

Mission

What We Do

P31 Labs builds open-source assistive technology for neurodivergent individuals. We create communication processing tools, sensory regulation devices, and executive function support systems that work offline, respect privacy, and form resilient mesh networks.

Local-First

Every system functions without cloud dependencies. Your data lives on your devices first. The cloud is for sync only, when you choose to connect.

Privacy-First

Type-level encryption. Zero-knowledge verification. No telemetry. No analytics. No surveillance. The mesh protects its own.

Mesh-Resilient

Tetrahedron topology. No single point of failure. Every node is a peer. When the neutral fails, the mesh holds.
