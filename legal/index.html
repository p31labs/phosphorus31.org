Privacy

Local-First

P31 Labs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All systems are local-first. No telemetry. No analytics. No surveillance.

Data Storage: All data lives on your devices first. Cloud sync is optional and encrypted.

Open Source: All code is publicly auditable. You can verify our privacy claims by reading the source.
