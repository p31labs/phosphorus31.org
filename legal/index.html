Legal

Licenses, privacy, and nonprofit status.

Licenses

Open Source

Software

Apache 2.0

All software code is licensed under the Apache License, Version 2.0. You may use, modify, and distribute freely. Patent grant included. No warranty.

What this means: Commercial use permitted. Modifications allowed. Distribution allowed. Patent protection included. Attribution required in derivative works.

Hardware

CERN-OHL-S

Hardware designs use the CERN Open Hardware Licence Strongly Reciprocal. Modifications must be shared under the same license.

What this means: You may manufacture, modify, and distribute hardware designs. If you modify and distribute, you must share your modifications under CERN-OHL-S. Commercial use permitted.

Research

CC-BY-4.0

Research publications and documentation use Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International. Attribution required.

What this means: You may use, share, and adapt research publications. Commercial use permitted. Attribution required. No additional restrictions.

Why Open Source? We believe assistive technology should be accessible to everyone. Open source ensures no vendor lock-in, no subscription fees, and no institutional dependency. The mesh holds because anyone can fork it, improve it, and deploy it.

Defensive Publication: All technical innovations are published to Zenodo with timestamped DOIs. This establishes prior art and prevents corporate patenting. We publish to protect freedom to operate, not to profit from IP.

Privacy

Local-First

P31 Labs does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All systems are local-first. No telemetry. No analytics. No surveillance.

Data Storage: All data lives on your devices first. Cloud sync is optional and encrypted.

No Tracking: We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services.

No Collection: We do not collect email addresses, usage statistics, or any personal information.

Open Source: All code is publicly auditable. You can verify our privacy claims by reading the source.

Nonprofit Status

501(c)(3) Formation

Current Status: P31 Labs is a Georgia nonprofit corporation in formation. Articles of Incorporation have been drafted. IRS Form 1023-EZ will be filed immediately after EIN issuance.

Fiscal Sponsorship: Until determination is received, P31 Labs operates under fiscal sponsorship through Hack Club HCB (The Hack Foundation, EIN 81-2908499). Donations are tax-deductible through this arrangement. Fiscal sponsor fee: 7% of revenue.

Timeline: EIN application in progress. Form 1023-EZ filing planned immediately after EIN issuance. Determination expected Q2 2026.

Board Structure: Board recruitment in progress. Seeking members with expertise in assistive technology, nonprofit governance, and lived experience with neurodivergence.

Financial Transparency: Once 501(c)(3) determination is received, all Form 990 filings will be publicly available. We believe in transparency and accountability.
