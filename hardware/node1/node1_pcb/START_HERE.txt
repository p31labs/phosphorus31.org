📋 KEY SPECIFICATIONS:

• Size: 51mm x 51mm (2" x 2")
• Layers: 2-layer FR4
• Finish: ENIG (Gold) on Matte Black solder mask
• MCU: ESP32-S3 (WiFi + BLE)
• Radio: LoRa 900MHz
• Inputs: 2x mechanical switches + rotary encoder
• Outputs: 5x RGB LEDs + haptic motor
• Power: USB-C + LiPo battery

Cost to build (5 boards): ~$80-125 USD

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⚠️  IMPORTANT BEFORE YOU ORDER:

1. Check LoRa frequency regulations in your country
   USA: 902-928 MHz (usually OK)
   EU: Different frequencies apply - verify first!

2. PCB MUST be ordered with:
   • Matte Black solder mask (not glossy!)
   • ENIG surface finish (for gold touch zones)
   
3. The exposed copper zones on bottom layer are INTENTIONAL
   (These are the tactile "worry stone" touch zones)

🎨 DESIGN PHILOSOPHY:

"The Board is the Product"

Most PCBs hide inside cases. Not this one.

This board celebrates electronics as art:
• Exposed components show the technology
• Matte black + gold create premium aesthetic
• Tactile touch zones engage the senses
• Geometric silkscreen patterns intrigue the eye
• Satisfying mechanical inputs provide feedback

It's designed to be displayed, not hidden.
