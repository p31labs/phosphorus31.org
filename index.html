Why

1 in 5 people are neurodivergent. Current tools are built for neurotypical workflows.

We build for neurodivergent people, by neurodivergent people. The tools don't exist yet. So we're building them.

When systems fail — divorce, job loss, benefits disruption — neurodivergent people lose their ground reference. There's no technology layer to catch them. P31 provides that layer.

Local-first. Privacy-first. No data harvesting. Everything works offline. No cloud dependency. Delta topology — mesh networking with no single point of failure. Dependencies need P31 — not the other way around.

Ephemeralization (Doing More With Less): P31 embodies Fuller's principle of ephemeralization — maximum efficiency with minimum resources. Our LoRa mesh operates on 0.350 kbps bandwidth (Whale Channel). Our devices run on battery power. Our code is optimized for minimal footprint. We compress, batch, and optimize until we can do everything with nothing. This is Bucky's principle, applied to assistive technology.

The Trimtab Principle: In Fullerian synergetics, the tetrahedron is the minimum stable structure. P31 builds on this geometry — four vertices, six edges, no single point of failure. Each node in the mesh is a trimtab: a small mechanism that can redirect the entire system toward resilience.
