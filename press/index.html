Brand Guidelines

Colors

  • Phosphorus Green #2ecc71
  • Calcium Blue #60a5fa
  • Void #050510
  • Light Background #f8fafc

Typography

Display: Outfit (Light 200, SemiBold 600, ExtraBold 800)

Monospace: JetBrains Mono (Light 300, Regular 400, Bold 700)

Serif accent: Instrument Serif (used in logos)

Usage

Always preserve clear space equal to the height of the "P" symbol. Do not stretch or recolor the logos. Use the green version on dark backgrounds, light version on light backgrounds.
