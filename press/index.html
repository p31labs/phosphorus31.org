P31 Labs

P31 Labs is a Georgia 501(c)(3) nonprofit in formation building open-source assistive technology for neurodivergent individuals. We create communication processing tools, sensory regulation devices, and executive function support systems that work offline, respect privacy, and form resilient mesh networks.

Our flagship product is Node One — an ESP32-S3-based handheld device for sensory regulation and off-grid communication. No internet required. No phone required. No permission required.

All software is Apache 2.0 licensed. All hardware designs are CERN-OHL-S. All research is published to Zenodo with timestamped DOIs. We believe in defensive publication, not patents.
