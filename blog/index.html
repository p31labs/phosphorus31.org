2025-11-01 · Philosophy

Why "Phosphorus-31"?

The name comes from the phosphorus-31 isotope — the only stable isotope of phosphorus, and a key component of hydroxyapatite (Ca₁₀(PO₄)₆(OH)₂), the mineral matrix of bone and teeth.

The connection: The founder's body cannot regulate calcium-phosphate balance due to hypoparathyroidism (since 2003). P31 builds technology that restores balance — starting with the mind.
The metaphor: Just as P-31 is the only stable isotope of phosphorus, P31 Labs builds the only stable systems — mesh-resilient, offline-first, privacy-protected. The mesh holds.
The mission: Restore balance. Build stability. Create systems that don't fail when the neutral is severed.
