Node One

The core mesh networking device — ESP32‑S3, LoRa, haptics, and privacy by design.

Technical Specifications

Node One v0.2
MCUESP32‑S3 (dual‑core Xtensa LX7, up to 240 MHz)
Memory512 KB SRAM, 8 MB PSRAM, 16 MB flash
WirelessLoRa 915 MHz (SX1262), BLE 5.0, NFC
HapticsDRV2605L with LRA motor
Display1.3" OLED (128x64) or E‑Ink (optional)
Power2000 mAh Li‑Po, USB‑C charging
Dimensions95 × 60 × 15 mm
Weight85 g
Operating temp-10°C to 50°C

Regulatory Compliance

Node One is designed as a wellness device under 21 CFR § 890.3710 (Powered communication system). It is not a medical device and does not require FDA clearance. Compliance with FCC Part 15 and Industry Canada RSS‑247 is pending.

Regulatory review in progress
